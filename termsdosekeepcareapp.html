DoseKeep Care Terms of Use

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Use of the App

DoseKeep Care provides medication reminder and logging tools. The user is responsible for entering accurate medication information and following guidance from qualified healthcare professionals.

No Medical Advice

The app does not diagnose, prescribe, recommend dose changes, check drug interactions, or replace professional medical advice. In an emergency, contact local emergency services.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are billed through the user's Apple ID and renew automatically unless cancelled according to Apple's subscription terms. Subscription management is available in the App Store account settings.

Limitations

Reminders depend on device settings, notification permissions, operating system behavior, time zone changes, and battery state. The app should not be the only safeguard for critical medication routines.
