Main Features

Night recording
Start a sleep session before bed and save a local audio recording.

Short clips
Review the night as a clip timeline instead of searching through hours of audio.

Sound labels
See labels for snore, sleep talk, cough, breathing, room noise, and unknown events.

Insights
Track snore minutes, talk clips, noise score, common labels, and night history.

Privacy

Noctory records only when you start a session. Recordings and generated night history are stored locally on your device and can be deleted in Settings.

Medical Notice

Noctory is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent sleep apnea or any medical condition.
