Resume Builder: JobCue

JobCue helps you build focused resume sections, compare your resume with a job description, prepare cover letter drafts, and unlock polished export when your application is ready.

What JobCue Does

  • Guides you through a short onboarding flow for your target role and job-search situation.
  • Lets free users create and edit up to three resume sections.
  • Uses AI actions to improve resume wording, run ATS-style checks, and draft cover letters.
  • Uses Apple StoreKit for optional subscriptions, restore purchases, and offer-code redemption.

Important Limits

JobCue does not guarantee interviews, job offers, recruiter replies, or ATS acceptance. AI output is drafting support and should be reviewed before sending.
