What the app does

  • Add expenses with amount, merchant, category, date, notes, and reimbursable status.
  • Attach receipt photos that stay on your device.
  • Track mileage for client trips and field work.
  • See monthly totals, category breakdowns, and missing-receipt warnings.
  • Export CSV and PDF reports when Pro is active.
