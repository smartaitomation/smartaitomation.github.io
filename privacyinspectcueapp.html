Privacy Policy

InspectCue is designed as an on-device inspection tool. Inspection content and draft assistance stay on your device.

At a glance

  • No InspectCue account is required.
  • Inspection records, photos, notes, signatures, and report branding are stored locally.
  • We do not sell personal information and do not use data for cross-app tracking or advertising.
  • Finding assistance and supported speech recognition run on-device. This release includes no product analytics or third-party diagnostic SDK.

Data handling

DataPurpose and handling
Assets, checklists, notes, findings, photos, signatures, and brandingStored on your device to provide inspections and PDF reports. InspectCue does not transmit or receive this content.
Speech inputRequested only when you tap record and processed on-device. If on-device recognition is not supported for the current device and locale, dictation remains unavailable and you can type instead.
Draft assistanceTyped or on-device-transcribed observation text is structured by code running inside InspectCue. The observation, equipment type, locale, result, photos, signatures, names, asset IDs, locations, and branding are not sent to us or an AI provider.

PurchasesApple processes subscription purchases, renewals, restorations, and offer codes. InspectCue receives entitlement and product information, not full payment-card details.
Support emailIf you contact us, we process the information you choose to provide to answer the request.

Permissions

Camera and photo access are requested only when you choose evidence capture. Microphone and speech recognition are requested only when you dictate. You can deny these permissions and continue with typed notes and manual findings. Permissions can be changed in iOS Settings.

Retention and deletion

Local records remain until you delete them or remove the app. Deleting the app may remove locally stored data unless it is included in an Apple device backup. Apple retains commerce data under its policies. Support email is retained only as reasonably needed for the request, security, and legal obligations.

Security and transfers

Inspection content is not transferred by InspectCue. If you choose Apple commerce or contact support, those external services use their own protected connections and may process data under their terms. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Children

InspectCue is an industrial productivity tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Your choices and rights

You can avoid optional assistance, deny permissions, delete local app data, and manage Apple subscriptions in your Apple Account. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to support information you voluntarily send. Contact us to exercise applicable rights.
