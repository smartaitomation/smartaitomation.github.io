Privacy Policy

InspectCue is designed as a local-first inspection tool. This policy explains what stays on your device, what may leave it, and your choices.

At a glance

  • No InspectCue account is required.
  • Inspection records, photos, notes, signatures, and report branding are stored locally.
  • We do not sell personal information and do not use data for cross-app tracking or advertising.
  • Optional diagnostics, product analytics, Apple commerce, speech recognition, and assisted finding drafts involve the limited data described below.

Data handling

DataPurpose and handling
Assets, checklists, notes, findings, photos, signatures, and brandingStored on your device to provide inspections and PDF reports. We do not receive this content unless you deliberately include limited observation text in a draft-assistance request.
Speech inputRequested only when you tap record. Apple speech services may process audio or transcription under Apple's terms and device settings. You can type instead.
Draft-assistance requestIf configured and requested, up to 4,000 characters of observation text, equipment type, and locale are sent to our HTTPS service and its AI processor to return an editable draft. Photos, signatures, names, asset IDs, locations, and branding are excluded by design.
Product analyticsOptional operational events such as onboarding completion, selected equipment category, inspection completion counts, paywall views, and purchase status may be processed by Mixpanel. Raw notes, photos, signatures, names, and observation text are excluded.
DiagnosticsOptional crash and performance details may be processed by Sentry to diagnose failures. Screenshot and view-hierarchy attachment are disabled; user content should not be sent.
PurchasesApple processes subscription purchases, renewals, restorations, and offer codes. InspectCue receives entitlement and product information, not full payment-card details.
Support emailIf you contact us, we process the information you choose to provide to answer the request.

Permissions

Camera and photo access are requested only when you choose evidence capture. Microphone and speech recognition are requested only when you dictate. You can deny these permissions and continue with typed notes and manual findings. Permissions can be changed in iOS Settings.

Retention and deletion

Local records remain until you delete them or remove the app. Deleting the app may remove locally stored data unless it is included in an Apple device backup. Service providers retain limited analytics, diagnostics, commerce, or draft-request data under their applicable settings and policies. Support email is retained only as reasonably needed for the request, security, and legal obligations.

Security and transfers

Network features use HTTPS. No system can guarantee absolute security. Service providers may process data in countries other than yours with their contractual and legal safeguards.

Children

InspectCue is an industrial productivity tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Your choices and rights

You can avoid optional assistance, deny permissions, stop analytics/diagnostics by using a build where they are not configured, delete local app data, and manage Apple subscriptions in your Apple Account. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing. Contact us to exercise applicable rights.
