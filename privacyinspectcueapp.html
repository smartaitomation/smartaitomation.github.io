Privacy Policy

InspectCue is designed as a local-first inspection tool. This policy explains what stays on your device, what may leave it, and your choices.

At a glance

  • No InspectCue account is required.
  • Inspection records, photos, notes, signatures, and report branding are stored locally.
  • We do not sell personal information and do not use data for cross-app tracking or advertising.
  • Apple commerce, speech recognition, and optional assisted finding drafts involve the limited data described below. Product analytics and third-party diagnostics are not enabled in this release.

Data handling

DataPurpose and handling
Assets, checklists, notes, findings, photos, signatures, and brandingStored on your device to provide inspections and PDF reports. We do not receive this content unless you deliberately include limited observation text in a draft-assistance request.
Speech inputRequested only when you tap record. Apple speech services may process audio or transcription under Apple's terms and device settings. You can type instead.

Photos, signatures, names, asset IDs, locations, and branding are excluded by design.

Permissions

Camera and photo access are requested only when you choose evidence capture. Microphone and speech recognition are requested only when you dictate. You can deny these permissions and continue with typed notes and manual findings. Permissions can be changed in iOS Settings.

Retention and deletion

Local records remain until you delete them or remove the app. Deleting the app may remove locally stored data unless it is included in an Apple device backup. Our draft proxy does not persist request bodies and keeps only a process-local salted network-rate-limit key for the active rate-limit window. OpenAI may retain API request and response content for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, unless law requires longer retention or shorter approved data-retention controls apply. Apple retains commerce data under its policies. Support email is retained only as reasonably needed for the request, security, and legal obligations.

Security and transfers

Network features use HTTPS. No system can guarantee absolute security. Service providers may process data in countries other than yours with their contractual and legal safeguards.

Children

InspectCue is an industrial productivity tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Your choices and rights

You can avoid optional assistance, deny permissions, delete local app data, and manage Apple subscriptions in your Apple Account. Product analytics and third-party diagnostics are not configured in this release. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing. Contact us to exercise applicable rights.
