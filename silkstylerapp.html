Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI hairstyle try-on work?

Our advanced AI technology uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of professional hairstyles. When you upload a photo, the AI analyzes facial features, head shape, and hair structure to apply hairstyles with precision. All processing happens locally on your device using Core ML for privacy and speed.

Do I need to create an account to use the app?

No! You can use most features of SilkStyler without creating an account. Basic hairstyle try-on and transformations are available immediately after download. Creating an account is optional and only required for saving your favorite looks and accessing premium features.

Is my photo data safe and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority. All photo processing happens directly on your device using Core ML technology. Photos are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. You have complete control over your images at all times.

What types of hairstyles are available?

SilkStyler offers a wide variety of hairstyles including long flowing waves, sleek bobs, elegant updos, trendy bangs (fringe), curly styles, straight styles, and more. You can also adjust hair length, color, and texture. Our library is regularly updated with new styles created by professional hairstylists.

Can I try different hair colors?

Yes! SilkStyler lets you experiment with various hair colors including natural shades (blonde, brunette, black, red) and even fantasy colors. This is perfect for trying out a new color before committing to a salon visit.

Can I use the app with any photo quality or lighting?

Yes! Our AI is designed to work with various photo qualities and lighting conditions. While better quality photos yield better results, the app can enhance and transform photos taken in different lighting scenarios.

Are there in-app purchases?

The app is free to download with basic features included. We offer optional in-app purchases for premium content: Premium Styles Pack ($2.99), Advanced Color Options ($4.99), and Pro Features ($9.99). No subscription required!

How accurate are the hairstyle previews?

Our AI provides highly realistic previews of how you would look with different hairstyles. While results are very accurate, actual salon results may vary based on your natural hair texture, thickness, and the skill of your stylist. The app is designed to help you visualize and explore options before making decisions.

What age restrictions apply to this app?

The app is rated 12+ due to mild/infrequent references to beauty and styling content. The content is suitable for teens and adults interested in hairstyling and beauty.
