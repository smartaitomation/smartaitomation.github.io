Privacy Policy

Confidence Journal: InnerProof · Effective date: June 3, 2026

Summary

InnerProof is designed as a private journaling app. Journal entries, onboarding choices, reminder settings, mood, local insights, and saved coach feedback are stored on your device unless you choose to request remote coach analysis.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following locally so the app can work:

  • onboarding profile choices, including language, problem, trigger, pattern, desired identity, feedback style, and first experiment;
  • journal entries, mood, saved feedback, proof bank, streak, and local insights;
  • reminder time and notification preference;
  • local premium entitlement state and free-feedback usage state.

Coach Analysis

Your journal entry and relevant onboarding context are sent to a remote AI analysis service only when you tap the feedback or analysis button. The app uses this response to show score, what works, weak spots, a stronger rewrite, a self-worth insight, and a next reflection prompt.

If you do not request coach analysis, your journal entries remain local in the app.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Subscription management, cancellation, and refund requests are handled by Apple through your Apple ID and App Store settings.

Tracking and Advertising

The app does not track users across apps or websites and does not sell personal data.

Safety Notice

InnerProof is a journaling and self-development tool. It is not therapy, medical care, diagnosis, crisis support, or emergency support.
