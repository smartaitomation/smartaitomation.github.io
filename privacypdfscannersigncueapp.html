Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

PDF Scanner SignCue is published by Ion Mosnoi. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Local documents

The app lets you scan, import, edit, sign, stamp, save, and export documents. Full PDFs, document pages, text overlays, dates, signatures, and stamps are stored on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Replicate background removal

If you tap Remove Background for a signature or stamp, the selected signature or stamp image can be sent to Replicate for background removal. Full PDFs and full document pages are not sent to Replicate for this feature.

Exports and sharing

When you export, save, share, or print a PDF, the file is created from your local document. You control where the exported PDF is sent.

No account required

The app does not require a sign-in account.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Tracking and diagnostics

The current app build does not track you across apps or websites. If analytics or diagnostics SDKs are enabled in a future build, this policy and App Store privacy answers will be updated.
