Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI makeup application work?

Our advanced AI technology uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of professional makeup looks. When you upload a photo, the AI analyzes facial features, skin tone, and bone structure to apply makeup with precision. All processing happens locally on your device using Core ML for privacy and speed.

Do I need to create an account to use the app?

No! You can use most features of gildAI without creating an account. Basic photo transformation and tutorials are available immediately after download. Creating an account is optional and only required for saving your favorite looks and accessing premium features.

Is my photo data safe and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority. All photo processing happens directly on your device using Core ML technology. Photos are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. You have complete control over your images at all times.

What types of makeup looks are available?

gildAI offers a wide variety of makeup styles including natural everyday looks, glamorous evening makeup, bold editorial styles, seasonal trends, and special occasion looks. Our library is regularly updated with new styles created by professional makeup artists.

Can I use the app with any photo quality or lighting?

Yes! Our AI is designed to work with various photo qualities and lighting conditions. While better quality photos yield better results, the app can enhance and transform photos taken in different lighting scenarios.

Are there in-app purchases?

The app is free to download with basic features included. We offer optional in-app purchases for premium content: Premium Styles Pack ($2.99), Advanced Tutorials ($4.99), and Pro Features ($9.99). No subscription required!

How do the tutorials work?

Each makeup transformation comes with personalized, step-by-step tutorials created by certified makeup artists. The tutorials show you exactly how to recreate the look in real life, including product recommendations and techniques tailored to your unique facial features.

What age restrictions apply to this app?

The app is rated 12+ due to mild/infrequent references to cosmetic products that may contain alcohol-based ingredients. The content is suitable for teens and adults interested in makeup and beauty.
