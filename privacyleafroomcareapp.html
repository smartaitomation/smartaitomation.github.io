LeafRoom Care Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Summary: LeafRoom Care does not collect personal data from the app. Plant names, optional plant photos, notes, schedules, and care logs are stored locally on the user's device.

Data stored on your device

The app may store plant names, plant photos selected by you, room names, light level, care schedules, notes, and care history. This information remains on your device and is used to run the plant tracker and reminders.

Photo use

Photos are used to show your plant in the app and to provide in-app plant type suggestions. Photos are not uploaded to our servers by LeafRoom Care.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Notifications

If you allow reminders, LeafRoom Care schedules local notifications on your device for plant care tasks. Notification preferences can be changed in iOS Settings.
