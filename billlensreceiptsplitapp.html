BillLens Receipt Split

Scan a restaurant receipt, assign items to people, add tax and tip, and see how much each person should pay.

What the app does

  • Imports receipt photos from the camera or photo library.
  • Uses GPT to extract receipt items into editable rows.
  • Lets you add people, reuse names from history, and assign items.
  • Calculates subtotal, tax, tip, and each person's total.
  • Stores past splits on device for later review.
