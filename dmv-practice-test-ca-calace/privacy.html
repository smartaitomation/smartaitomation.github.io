Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

CalAce DMV is an offline-first study app for California DMV practice. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

Data stored on your device

The app stores study progress, selected language, practice answers, saved questions, and optional notes locally on your device. This data is not sent to our servers.

Purchases

Subscriptions and offer-code redemption are handled by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details.

Tracking and analytics

The app does not use third-party advertising, tracking, analytics SDKs, camera access, photo library access, or location access.
