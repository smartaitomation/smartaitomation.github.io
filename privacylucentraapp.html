Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

What We Collect

Lucentra stores onboarding answers, selected language, dream entries, interpretation results, and report stats on your device. No account is required.

Dream Text And Audio

When you tap Analyze, the current dream text, selected language, and relevant onboarding answers may be sent to OpenAI, LLC for AI interpretation. When you record audio and tap Transcribe, the current audio file may be sent to OpenAI, LLC for transcription. Audio is recorded only after you tap the record button.

Lucentra uses this data only to provide the requested interpretation or transcription in the app. It is not used for third-party advertising.

Third-Party Processing

Lucentra shares dream text, dream audio, and related prompt context with OpenAI, LLC only when you request an AI feature. OpenAI is expected to provide the same or equal protection for user data as described in this policy and required by App Store Review Guidelines.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Diagnostics And Analytics

If diagnostics or analytics services are enabled, limited crash, performance, and interaction data may be collected to improve stability and product quality. This data is not used for third-party advertising.

Wellness Notice

Lucentra is a dream journal and reflection tool. It does not provide therapy, diagnosis, medical advice, predictions, or guaranteed meanings.

Retention, Consent, And Deletion

Dream history and app preferences remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. You can withdraw consent for AI processing by not using Analyze or Transcribe. To request help with deletion or privacy questions, contact us using the details below.
