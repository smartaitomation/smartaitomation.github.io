Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026. Mileage Tracker OdoCue is designed as a local-first mileage tracker.

Summary

OdoCue does not collect personal data, does not create accounts, does not use GPS tracking, does not use advertising SDKs, and does not sync your mileage log to a cloud service.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores mileage entries, vehicle names, plate text if you enter it, mileage rates, notes, preferences, language, country, unit settings, and generated export files locally on your device.

If you share a CSV or PDF export, that share action is controlled by iOS and the destination you choose.

Subscriptions

OdoCue uses Apple StoreKit for subscriptions, purchase restoration, and offer code redemption. Apple processes purchases and may provide subscription status to the app. We do not receive your payment card details.

No Location Tracking

OdoCue does not request location permission and does not track trips automatically. Trip distances are entered manually by you.
