Subscription Help

ReadCue Premium is managed through Apple. You can purchase, cancel, or manage a subscription from your Apple ID settings. In the app, use Restore Purchases if you already subscribed and Redeem Offer Code if you have an Apple offer code.

Privacy

Your saved library, notes, reading sessions, and onboarding choices are stored locally by the app. If you choose AI extraction, the text, image, or audio you provide is sent to the configured extraction service only to identify book metadata.
