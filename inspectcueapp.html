Quick answers

Where is my inspection data stored?

Assets, checklist results, notes, attached photos, findings, signatures, and saved reports are stored locally on your device. InspectCue does not require an account.

Can I inspect without a connection?

Yes. Checklists, evidence, findings, signatures, reports, on-device dictation where supported, and assisted finding drafts work without sending inspection content to a server. Apple purchases, offer codes, and support links require a connection.

What does draft assistance do?

It restructures your typed or on-device-transcribed observation into an editable draft directly on your device. It cannot complete a check, sign a record, or determine compliance. Review every word, action, and severity before adding it.

How do I restore a subscription?

Open Settings in InspectCue and choose Restore purchases. Use the same Apple Account that made the purchase.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open iOS Settings, tap your name, choose Subscriptions, then InspectCue.
