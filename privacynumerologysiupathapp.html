Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This policy explains how Numerology: SiuPath handles information. The app is operated by Ion Mosnoi.

Information Collected in the App

You may enter or choose your birthday, gender, selected language, focus area, problem or feeling answers, timeline, blocker, repeating pattern, guidance style, yes/no answers, desired answer type, partner birthday, partner gender, and typed follow-up questions. The app also calculates numerology numbers such as life path, destiny, birthday, attitude, personal year, compatibility number, and match score.

This information is collected when you type it, select it during onboarding, select partner details, ask a question, or use app features. The app stores profile details, partner details, generated readings, generated answers, selected language, onboarding completion, AI sharing permission, and purchase state on your device so the app can show your readings and unlock premium features.

How Information Is Used

The app uses this information to create entertainment numerology readings, compatibility readings, section previews, follow-up answers, subscription access, restore purchases, support responses, and app reliability improvements.

AI Processing and Third Parties

Enhanced AI answers are optional. Before any profile or question context is sent to a third-party AI service, the app shows an in-app permission message and asks you to tap Allow AI Answers.

If you allow AI data sharing, the app may send the following to OpenAI directly or through Smart Automation SRL's backend proxy: birthday, gender, selected focus, problem and feeling answers, timeline, blocker, repeating pattern, guidance style, yes/no answers, desired answer type, partner birthday and gender for compatibility, calculated numerology numbers, reading summaries or section context, selected language, and typed follow-up questions. This information is sent over HTTPS and is used to generate entertainment numerology text and answers.

If you do not allow AI data sharing, the app uses local numerology text and does not send this profile or question context to OpenAI or the backend proxy. You can turn AI data sharing off in Settings.

Do not enter sensitive medical, legal, financial, confidential, or emergency information into the app. Numerology: SiuPath is for entertainment and reflection only.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We receive purchase status needed to unlock premium features, but we do not receive your full payment card details.

Diagnostics

If crash reporting or system diagnostics are enabled, diagnostic data such as device type, iOS version, app version, crash logs, and technical errors may be processed to improve reliability. Diagnostics are used for debugging and app stability.

Data Sharing

We share information only as needed to operate the app: Apple processes purchases through StoreKit; OpenAI and/or Smart Automation SRL's backend proxy may process AI prompts only after you allow AI data sharing; support email providers process messages you send to support; diagnostic providers may process crash or error information if enabled.

Tracking and Sale

We do not sell personal information. The app is not designed to track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising.

Retention and Choices

Most app profile and reading data is stored on your device and can be changed by restarting onboarding or deleting the app. You can turn off AI data sharing in Settings. To ask support questions or request help with information sent through support, contact us using the details below.

Children

The app is for general audiences but is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will help remove it where possible.
