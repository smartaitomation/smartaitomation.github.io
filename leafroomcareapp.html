LeafRoom Care

LeafRoom Care is an iPhone and iPad app for apartment plant care, plant type suggestions, watering reminders, and local care history.

What the app does

Track each indoor plant with a photo, room, light level, care notes, and schedule.
Log watering, fertilizing, misting, rotating, and skipped care tasks.
Choose a plant manually or use a photo to receive likely plant type suggestions.
Use reminders so apartment plants get care at the right time.
