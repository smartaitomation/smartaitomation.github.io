Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

What We Collect

Slabeste cu Gust stores onboarding answers, selected language, meal tracking status, streak data, and an optional motivation photo on your device. If Sentry is configured, diagnostic crash and error data may be collected to improve stability.

Photos

The motivation photo is used to display a weight-loss preview inside the app. The current app version keeps the photo locally unless a future image-generation service is explicitly configured.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Health Notice

The app provides meal planning and tracking guidance. It is not medical advice. Consult a qualified professional before starting a diet if you have medical conditions or special nutritional needs.
