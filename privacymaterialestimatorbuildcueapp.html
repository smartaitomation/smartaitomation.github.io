Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Information Stored in the App

Material Estimator BuildCue stores calculator inputs, saved projects, quote summaries, selected language, unit preferences, waste defaults, and AI usage counters on your device in the current version.

AI Notes, Voice, and Photos

The app can turn job notes into material estimates. Text notes are processed in the app's estimator flow. Voice input uses user-approved speech capture, and photo input is user-selected from the photo picker for readable text extraction. Do not enter sensitive personal data into job notes.

Subscriptions

Purchases, subscription status, trials, refunds, and offer codes are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

If analytics or diagnostics are configured in a release build, limited app events and crash information may be used to improve stability and product quality. The app does not use third-party advertising tracking.

Sharing

When you share a quote summary, you choose the destination through the iOS share sheet. The app does not publish your project data publicly.
