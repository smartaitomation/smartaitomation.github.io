Main Features

Morning plan

Alarm Reliability

WakeCue schedules local notifications and does not replace Apple's Clock app or a system alarm. Keep notifications enabled and confirm device volume, Focus settings, battery state, and alarm details before relying on an alert.

Subscription Help

WakeCue Premium is managed through Apple. Use Restore Purchases in the app if you already subscribed. Apple handles billing, cancellation, refunds, renewals, and offer-code redemption.
