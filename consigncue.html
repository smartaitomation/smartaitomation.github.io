ConsignCue

ConsignCue is an iOS app for consignment stores, thrift shops, antique booths, and resellers that need one place for intake, payouts, inventory, and daily closeout reports.

What ConsignCue Helps With

  • Create consignor profiles with payout rates and notes.
  • Record intake items with owner, price, category, SKU, and status.
  • Calculate payout and shop share when an item sells.
  • Track daily sales totals, payouts due, and end-of-day cash drawer counts.
  • Review active, sold, paid, returned, and expired inventory in one workflow.

Subscriptions

Premium features are managed through Apple. If you already subscribed, use Restore Purchases in the app. You can manage or cancel a subscription from your Apple ID subscription settings.
