Main Features

Inventory
Save craft supplies with quantity, color, location, category, notes, and low-stock status.

Projects
Plan works in progress and connect materials to each project before shopping again.

Wishlist
Keep future supplies separate from the stash you already own.

AI Quick Add
Turn short stash notes into structured items with usage limits for free and Premium users.

Subscriptions

The app is free to download. Premium subscriptions unlock higher inventory and project limits, larger weekly AI Quick Add limits, and premium craft-tracking workflow. Purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and offer codes are handled by Apple StoreKit.

Languages

CraftCue includes in-app language selection and App Store metadata support for 32 languages where available in App Store Connect.
