Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Summary

Alarm Clock WakeCue does not require an account. Alarms, onboarding choices, language preference, and weekly feature-usage counters are stored locally on your device.

WakeCue does not sell personal information and does not use advertising tracking.

Alarm and Routine Data

Alarm times, labels, repeat days, tones, challenges, snooze settings, and routine preferences remain on the device unless you choose to share information through another app or an Apple service.

Voice-Style Setup

WakeCue does not record audio and does not request microphone access. You may type text or use the iOS keyboard's dictation feature. WakeCue receives the resulting text and parses it locally into an editable alarm draft.

Notifications

The app requests notification permission when needed to schedule local alarm alerts. Notification requests are stored and delivered by iOS according to your device settings.

Purchases

Purchases, renewals, cancellation, refunds, offer codes, and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment card details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

This release does not enable third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote crash-reporting services.

Support Communications

If you contact support, we receive the information you choose to include in your email or call and use it to answer your request.
