Main Features

Voice or text capture
Record a workplace update or type it manually when voice capture is unavailable.

Structured handovers
Review extracted tasks, urgency, ownership, status, and categories before saving.

Open-task relay
Carry unresolved work into the next handover and mark it complete when the team closes it.

Acknowledgement and PDF
Record who received a handover and export a clear report for operational use.

Subscription Help

NoteRelay Premium subscriptions are purchased and managed through Apple. Use Restore Purchases in the app if you already subscribed. Use Redeem Offer Code for an eligible Apple offer code. You can manage or cancel a subscription in your Apple Account subscription settings.

Privacy and Control

Saved handovers and attached photos remain in the app's local storage unless you choose to share an exported report. You can clear app data from Settings. Review structured results before relying on or sharing them.
