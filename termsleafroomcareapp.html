LeafRoom Care Terms of Use

Use of the app

LeafRoom Care is provided for personal home plant organization, care reminders, and general plant tracking. The app does not provide professional botanical, agricultural, or medical advice.

Plant suggestions

Photo-based plant type suggestions are informational and may be inaccurate. Users should confirm plant type and care instructions before relying on a schedule.

Subscriptions

LeafRoom Care may offer auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple. Payment is charged to the Apple Account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Subscriptions can be managed in Apple Account settings.

Availability

Features may change over time. We may update, pause, or remove app features as needed to maintain the service.
