Clovio Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026.

Clovio helps users create a closet database, outfit matches, shop-match feedback, body profile measurements, and try-on previews.

Data You Provide

You may add clothing photos, shop item photos, body profile photos, measurements, clothing notes, and outfit or shopping text prompts. This data is used to provide the app features you request.

Storage

The app is designed to store closet items, usage counters, body profile values, and generated previews on the device. If cloud AI processing is enabled in a future build, photos and prompts may be sent to the selected AI provider only to generate the requested analysis or preview.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled by Apple StoreKit. Clovio does not receive your full payment card details.
