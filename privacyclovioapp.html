Clovio Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026.

Clovio helps users create a closet database, outfit matches, shop-match feedback, body profile estimates, and try-on previews.

Data You Provide

You may choose to add clothing photos, shop item photos, body profile photos, body profile values, clothing notes, outfit prompts, and shopping prompts. Clovio collects this data only when you enter text in the app or choose a camera, photo-library, or upload button.

Storage

The app is designed to store closet items, clothing attributes, usage counters, body profile values, selected photos, generated previews, and your AI-processing consent choice on the device.

AI Processing

Before Clovio sends personal data to a third-party AI service, the app displays an in-app consent screen that explains the transfer and asks for your permission. If you do not allow AI processing, local closet and outfit features remain available.

When you allow AI processing and request AI closet detection or AI try-on previews, Clovio may send the photos you selected, cropped clothing images, body profile values, closet item details, and text prompts for that request to Replicate, Inc. Replicate processes the data only to return the requested clothing analysis, item attributes, bounding boxes, or generated image. Clovio does not use this data for third-party advertising or tracking.

Clovio expects third-party AI providers used for these requests to provide the same or equal protection for the data they process for the requested feature.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled by Apple StoreKit. Clovio does not receive your full payment card details.
