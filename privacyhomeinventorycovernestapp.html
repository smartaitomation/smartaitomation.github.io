Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Summary

Home Inventory: CoverNest stores inventory items, rooms, receipt notes, serial numbers, warranty dates, repair reminders, photos, onboarding choices, reports, and language preference on your device.

CoverNest does not sell user content and does not use household inventory data for advertising tracking.

User Content

If you use LLM extraction, the receipt text, item notes, or optional image-related text you provide may be processed to create editable item fields. This processing is used for app functionality and not for advertising tracking.

PDF reports are created from the information you enter in the app. You control when to export or share those reports.

Photos and Local Storage

Item, receipt, warranty, and serial-number photos remain local on the device unless you export, share, back up, or otherwise transmit them through your own device settings or Apple services.

Subscriptions

Purchases, renewals, cancellation, refunds, offer codes, and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment card details.

Analytics

The app may use Mixpanel analytics to understand app launches, onboarding completion, paywall events, StoreKit loading, LLM extraction usage, PDF export events, and settings actions. Analytics events include the app name parameter and do not include payment card details.
