App Description

SnackGuard helps you maintain healthy 4-hour gaps between meals so your insulin can reset, metabolism can recover, and weight loss feels natural. Log every snack or meal, get gentle reminders, and use the one-tap panic button to record cravings before they turn into unplanned snacks.

Perfect for:

  • People practicing time-spaced eating or glucose control
  • Anyone wanting to reduce mindless snacking
  • Those tracking weight-loss progress without strict calorie counting
  • Busy schedules that need simple, reliable meal spacing
  • Users who want quick logging and visual progress

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 4-hour gap work?
SnackGuard starts a timer every time you log food. You can see remaining time, get reminders, and reset if you intentionally start a new meal early.
Do I need to count calories?
No. SnackGuard focuses on timing and awareness. You can add notes or photos, but calorie tracking is optional.
What is the panic button?
It is a one-tap logger for cravings or unexpected urges. Capture the moment and reset your focus without breaking your streak.
Is this medical advice?
No. SnackGuard offers habit support and reminders. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical or dietary advice.
Does it work offline?
Core logging and timers work offline. Analytics sync when you reconnect.
