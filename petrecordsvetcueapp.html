Subscription Help

VetCue Premium is managed by Apple through StoreKit. You can purchase, cancel, restore, or manage a subscription from your Apple ID settings. In the app, use Restore Purchases if you already subscribed and Redeem Offer Code if you have an Apple offer code.

Important Notice

VetCue organizes owner-provided pet records. It does not diagnose, treat, prevent, or replace veterinary care. Always confirm medical details with a licensed veterinarian.
