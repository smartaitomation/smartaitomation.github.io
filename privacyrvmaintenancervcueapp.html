Privacy Policy

RVCue is designed as a local-first RV maintenance tool. This policy explains what stays on your device, what may leave it, and your choices.

At a glance

  • No RVCue account is required.
  • Your rig profile, checklists, maintenance tasks, service records, attachment references, and saved briefs are stored locally.
  • We do not sell personal information or use RVCue data for cross-app tracking or advertising.
  • Optional AI organization, Apple commerce, selected photos, and support email involve only the data described below.

Data handling

DataPurpose and handling
Rig profile, maintenance tasks, inspections, service history, and saved briefsStored on your device to provide RVCue. We do not receive this local content unless you deliberately include text in an AI service-brief request or support email.
Receipt, manual, and service photosSelected only when you use the attachment workflow and stored through the app's local record. They are not included in AI service-brief requests in version 1.0.
AI service-brief requestOnly when you tap the AI action, the concern, observations, completed checks, and limited rig context are sent over HTTPS to the configured AI service to return an editable structured brief. Critical safety signals are stopped on device before the request. Do not enter secrets or personal information you do not want processed.
PurchasesApple processes subscription purchases, renewals, restorations, and offer codes. RVCue receives product and entitlement information, not full payment-card details.
Support emailIf you contact us, we process the information you choose to provide to answer your request.

Permissions

Camera and photo-library access are requested only when you choose to attach a receipt, manual page, or service photo. You can deny these permissions and continue using checklists, maintenance tasks, records without photos, and service briefs. Change permissions in iOS Settings.

Retention and deletion

Local data remains until you reset RVCue, delete individual content where available, or remove the app. Removing the app may remove local data unless it is included in an Apple device backup. AI providers, Apple, and email providers retain limited information under their applicable settings, contracts, and policies.

Security, transfers, and children

Network features use HTTPS, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Providers may process data in countries other than yours with applicable safeguards. RVCue is an ownership and maintenance utility and is not directed to children; we do not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Your choices and rights

You can use the on-device brief instead of AI, deny photo permissions, reset local app data, and manage subscriptions in your Apple Account. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing. Contact us to exercise applicable rights.
