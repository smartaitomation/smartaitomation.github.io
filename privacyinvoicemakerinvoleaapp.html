Information Stored in the App

Invoice Maker Involea stores invoice drafts, estimates, client details, business profile settings, selected language, and document preferences locally on your device in the current version.

When you export or share a PDF, you choose the destination through the iOS share sheet. The app does not publish documents publicly.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Diagnostics

If diagnostic services are configured for a release build, limited crash or error information may be used to improve stability. The app does not use third-party tracking for advertising.
