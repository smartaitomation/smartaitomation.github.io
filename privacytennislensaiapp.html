Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

What We Collect

TennisLens AI stores onboarding choices, selected stroke, player level, camera angle, selected language, analysis reports, and premium status on your device. If crash reporting is configured, limited diagnostic and error data may be collected to improve stability.

Videos

When you choose to analyze a video, the app may send the first 30 seconds of that video to Google Gemini for swing detection and technique analysis if the AI service is configured. The app does not provide social sharing, public posting, or user-to-user messaging.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

No Tracking

TennisLens AI does not use your data to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Training Notice

The app provides video-analysis and training guidance. It is not medical advice, injury diagnosis, or a replacement for in-person coaching and safe practice supervision.
