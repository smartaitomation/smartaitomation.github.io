Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This policy applies to ConsignCue, an iOS app by Smart Automation SRL.

Data Stored On Your Device

ConsignCue is a local-first app. Consignor names, contact notes, payout rates, item intake records, sale status, daily totals, drawer counts, onboarding answers, language choice, and subscription state used by the app are stored on your device.

ConsignCue does not require an account and does not upload your shop records to Smart Automation SRL servers as part of the core workflow.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Analytics And Diagnostics

The current app configuration does not enable third-party advertising tracking.

The review-ready build of ConsignCue is configured without active Mixpanel analytics or Sentry crash reporting credentials. If a future version enables diagnostics or analytics, this policy will be updated to reflect that behavior.
