Common Questions

Does OdoCue track my location?
No. OdoCue is manual-first and does not use GPS tracking.

Where is my data stored?
Your mileage records, vehicles, rates, notes, and settings are stored locally on your device.

How do exports work?
Premium users can create CSV and PDF reports from the Export tab and share them with an accountant, employer, or their own files.

Is OdoCue tax advice?
No. OdoCue organizes mileage records, but tax rules vary by country and personal situation.
