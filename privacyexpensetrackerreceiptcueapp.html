Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Expense Tracker ReceiptCue is published by Ion Mosnoi. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Local expense data

The app lets you enter expense amounts, merchants, categories, dates, notes, receipt photos, and mileage. This data is stored on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Exports

If you create a CSV or PDF export, the file is created locally. You control whether to share it with an accountant, client, or another app.

No account or bank sync

ReceiptCue does not require an account and does not connect to bank accounts, cards, or financial institutions.

Photos

Receipt photos you select are used only for your local expense records. Do not attach receipts that you do not want stored on your device.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Tracking and diagnostics

The current app build does not track you across apps or websites and does not send expense or receipt data to third-party analytics services.
