Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Summary

Contractor Estimate Bidcue stores estimates, templates, line items, change orders, signatures, onboarding choices, and language preference on your device.

Bidcue does not sell user content and does not use job data for advertising tracking.

User Content

If you use AI estimate drafting, the job notes and optional job photos you provide may be processed to create editable estimate lines. This processing is used for app functionality and not for advertising tracking.

PDF exports and signatures are created from the information you enter in the app.

Subscriptions

Purchases, renewals, cancellation, offer codes, and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment card details.

Analytics

The app may use Mixpanel analytics to understand app launches, onboarding completion, paywall events, StoreKit loading, AI draft usage, and PDF export events. Analytics events include the app name parameter and do not include payment card details.
