Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Habit Widget GoalCue is local-first. Goals, habits, journal entries, onboarding answers, streaks, and widget state are stored on your device. Widget data is shared with the app's widget extension through the app group container.

Data Collection

The current app build does not send your goals, habits, journal text, or onboarding answers to Smart Automation SRL or third-party analytics providers.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Advertising

The app does not use third-party advertising tracking.
