Subscriptions and Purchases

  • Premium access is offered through Apple StoreKit auto-renewable subscriptions.
  • To restore a purchase, open DateTalk Trainer, go to Settings or the paywall, and tap Restore purchases.
  • To cancel or manage billing, open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions.
  • For App Store billing refunds, use Apple’s purchase history and refund flow.

Privacy and Safety

DateTalk Trainer is a private educational tool. It does not send messages for you, create user accounts, upload practice replies, or collect data for tracking.

The app is intended to help users practice respectful communication judgment. Do not use the training content to harass, pressure, deceive, or manipulate anyone.
