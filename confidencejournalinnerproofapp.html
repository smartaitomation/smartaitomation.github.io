What the App Does

Today
Guides one nightly entry through four prompts, one question at a time.

Coach feedback
After saving an entry, the app can analyze it and show stronger wording, weak spots, and a next reflection prompt.

History
Saved entries and saved feedback stay available locally unless you delete them.

Insights
Shows streak, proof bank, common trigger, mood pattern, weekly summary, and behavioral experiment prompts.

Subscriptions and Purchases

  • The first coach feedback is free per app install or profile.
  • Premium access unlocks more coach feedback, regeneration, and deeper weekly summaries.
  • To restore a purchase, open the app's Settings or paywall and tap Restore Purchases.
  • To cancel or manage billing, open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions.

Safety Notice
