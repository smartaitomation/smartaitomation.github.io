Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Information Stored in the App

CraftCue stores craft inventory items, project plans, wishlist entries, onboarding answers, selected language, subscription status, and AI usage counters on your device in the current version.

AI Quick Add

The current AI Quick Add flow structures short text notes into craft inventory items inside the app. Do not enter sensitive personal, financial, health, or account information into item notes.

Subscriptions and Offer Codes

Purchases, subscription status, free trials, refunds, renewals, cancellations, and offer-code redemption are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

If analytics or diagnostics are enabled in a release build, limited app events, app version, language, device information, and crash details may be processed by services such as Mixpanel or Sentry to improve product quality. CraftCue does not use third-party advertising tracking.

Device Permissions

The current app does not require camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, precise location, or health data access for its core inventory, project, wishlist, and settings features.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete local app data by removing the app from your device. You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID App Store subscription settings.
