Privacy Policy

DateTalk Trainer · Effective date: May 20, 2026

Summary

DateTalk Trainer is designed as a private training app. The current app does not create accounts, upload practice replies, sell personal data, or track users across apps and websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following locally on your device so the training experience works:

  • onboarding choices, selected level, and training goal;
  • quiz attempts, selected answers, accuracy, missed tags, and progress;
  • practice reply text while you are using the practice screen;
  • language selection and local premium unlock state.

Practice Replies

Text typed into practice mode is used to show feedback in the app. The current app does not upload practice replies, send messages, create user-to-user communication, or contact anyone on your behalf.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Subscription management, cancellation, and refund requests are handled by Apple through your Apple ID and App Store settings.

Tracking and Advertising

The app does not track users across apps or websites and does not use data for advertising tracking.

Children

DateTalk Trainer is intended for adults and is not directed to children.
