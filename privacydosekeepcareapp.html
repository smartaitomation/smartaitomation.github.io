DoseKeep Care Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

DoseKeep Care is designed as a local reminder and medication log. Medication names, care profiles, reminder times, refill counts, and dose history are stored on the user's device.

Information Stored

The app may store care profile names, relationships, medication names, dosage text, reminder times, refill counts, notes, and dose log entries locally on the device.

Notifications

If the user enables reminders, the app schedules local notifications on the device. Notification permission can be changed in iOS Settings.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment card details.

Diagnostics

If diagnostics are enabled in a production build, the app may send technical error information needed to debug crashes or StoreKit product loading issues. Diagnostics should not include medication names, notes, prescriptions, or other sensitive medical content.
