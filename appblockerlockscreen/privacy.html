Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how App Blocker: Lockscreen handles information.

Data Collection

The current app is designed as a local-first productivity tool. We do not collect personal data from the app, do not create user accounts, and do not use third-party tracking or analytics SDKs.

On-Device Information

The app may store the following on your device so the app can work:

  • Selected task preferences and custom task names
  • Selected app/category labels used to describe your blocks
  • Current lock state, timers, and completion stats
  • Selected app language and subscription status shown by StoreKit

This information stays on your device unless a future version clearly adds sync or account features and updates this policy.

Screen Time Permission

The app uses Apple's Screen Time frameworks so you can select apps, categories, or websites to block. iOS handles this permission and selection UI. App Blocker: Lockscreen does not read messages, passwords, browsing content, or content inside blocked apps.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Children

The app is a productivity tool and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
