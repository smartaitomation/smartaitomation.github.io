App Blocker: Lockscreen

Block distracting apps until one small task is complete. Choose apps or categories with Apple's Screen Time picker, start a lock, and finish the task before returning to the distraction.

Open on the App Store Get Support

Task-first blocking

Pick a small action like clearing your desk, opening the work, drinking water, or writing one sentence before apps reopen.

Screen Time selection

Use Apple's privacy-safe picker for apps, categories, and websites. The app does not read messages, passwords, or app content.

Private by default

Task preferences, lock state, and completion stats are stored locally in the app.
