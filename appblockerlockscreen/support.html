Common Help

  • Use Settings in the app to restore purchases.
  • Use Allow Screen Time Access to let iOS show the app/category picker.
  • If a block does not apply, reopen the app, confirm Screen Time permission, and select apps again.
  • Screen Time controls are managed by iOS. The app cannot read messages, passwords, or app content.
